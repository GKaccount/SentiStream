About Us

Welcome to SentiStream!

At SentiStream, our mission is to provide insightful and meaningful analysis of YouTube comments through cutting-edge sentiment analysis technology. We understand that YouTube is a vibrant platform where millions of users share their thoughts, opinions, and experiences daily. However, sifting through these comments to understand public sentiment can be overwhelming. That's where we come in.

Our Purpose

Our website is designed to help content creators, marketers, researchers, and everyday users gain a deeper understanding of how audiences feel about videos, channels, and trending topics on YouTube. By leveraging advanced natural language processing (NLP) algorithms and machine learning techniques, we provide detailed sentiment analysis reports that categorize comments as positive, negative, or neutral.

Key Features

  • Real-time Analysis: Get up-to-date sentiment analysis on the latest YouTube comments.
  • Comprehensive Reports: Dive into detailed sentiment breakdowns with visual graphs and statistics.
  • Keyword Insights: Discover common keywords and phrases used in comments to understand trending topics and audience interests.
  • Customizable Settings: Tailor the analysis to specific videos, channels, or timeframes for more targeted insights.
  • User-friendly Interface: Enjoy an intuitive and easy-to-navigate platform that makes sentiment analysis accessible to everyone.
